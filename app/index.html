At the Hertfordshire, golf isn't just a game; it's a way of life. We have dedicated ourselves to the extraordinary game of golf since the year 1995. Our magnificent 18-hole golf course was designed by renowned course designer Jack Niklaus II, who inherited his exceptional skills, talent and dedication from his celebrated father Jack Nicklaus I.

The course at the Hertfordshire remains true to Jack II's vision. Our course offers a variety of natural experiences to players, making it perfect for professionals and amateurs with different skill sets and techniques. Massive bunkers intersperse the fairways, and the final stretch of the course has challenging water hazards, making the course quite demanding.

We have a massive driving range that is open to Club members and non-members, and you are welcome to visit us any time and get in a game. Whether you wish to play to unwind or get in serious practice for a tournament, rest assured, Hertfordshire's Grade II golf course is perfect for your ambitions and aspirations.

Mission statement

Our mission at the Hertfordshire Golf & Country Club is to offer our guests and visitors the most outstanding golfing experience in a safe and welcoming environment. We aim to always maintain contemporary and easy-to-use golfing facilities, which can add to the sporting experience.

We also wish to offer guests a variety of lifestyle facilities that function as a haven from their stressful lives. We seek to do this by providing premium guest service and modern amenities through the support of a dedicated, highly-trained and motivated staff.

Our priority has always been guest safety and comfort, and we will continue to remain committed to our guestsâ€™ welfare for years to come.

Our core values

  • Customer service: We will offer a premium guest experience through focused, sincere care.
  • Respect: We will be welcoming and accepting of all guests, visitors and employees, irrespective of their ethnicities, age and occupations.
  • Integrity: We will be honest and transparent in everything we do and all that we offer.
  • Creativity: We will strive to offer unique, unforgettable experiences both on the course and in the mansion, through out-of-the-box services.
  • Consistency: We will always maintain the highest standards of quality and provide consistent and reliable service for years to come.
  • Privacy: We will maintain the trust and confidentiality of our guests and visitors through the provision of dedicated digital and physical data protection and privacy.
